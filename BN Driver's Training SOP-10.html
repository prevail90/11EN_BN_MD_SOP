2-10 LICENSE INSTRUCTOR

The License Instructor will-

a.

Have overall responsibility for conducting phase II equipment training, to include 

the classroom equipment introduction, and all hands-on training (see para 4-3a, AR 
600-55).

b.

Successfully complete the License Instructor and Examiner distance learning 

course Black Board Learning Management System through ATRRS registration.

c.

Have technical knowledge of the equipment.

d.

Be appointed in writing by the Company Commander.

e.

Be a SGT/E-5 or above.

f.

Possess a license for the vehicle or equipment on which they will train.

g.

Prepare their Company personnel’s DA Form 348 and annotate all entries in 

GCSS-Army system.

h.

Ensure vehicle and equipment operators are properly licensed and trained IAW 

AR 600-55.

i.

Ensure all records and forms are maintained IAW AR 600-55.
